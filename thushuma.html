[ VISUAL IDENTITY ]

THE NAME /

Derived from the frisian word for "home," 'thús' embodies the essence of a deeply personal space. it is a reflection of connection, comfort, and storytelling: a sanctuary where life unfolds in harmony with artistry.


THE LOGO /

a representation of a space, derived from type. concise yet evocative, to match the brand.


visit the website here.

[ BRANDING ]

IDENTITY /

the identity of the brand was developed to showcase the sophistication and timelessness of the works of art being showcased.

The branding draws inspiration from the classic elegance and distinction of serif typefaces, chosen to evoke a sense of luxury.

The delicate curves and bold strokes of the serifs breathe life into the brand, evoking a sense of heritage authority, yet remaining modern.

[ OUTCOME ]

WEBSITE /

The digital ecosystem for Thús Huma was built as an extension of the showspace itself. The website navigates through discovery rather than catalogue logic, with section headings and copy that carry the same tonal register as the space: here & now, gathered worlds, an everyday life of design.

COLLATERALS /

Print for Thús Huma was designed to be kept, a keepsake over just paper. The showspace brochure holds directions alongside design philosophy, and visit information alongside curatorial intent, each given equal weight.
